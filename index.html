About the foundation

Built from lived experience and a belief in practical support.

MW Foundation was created to support vulnerable children and youth, both boys and girls, by giving them access to education materials, guidance, and basic needs. The work is grounded in a simple conviction: one act of support can change the direction of a young life.

The foundation's goal is to become the helping hand that every vulnerable child deserves, especially where financial hardship, limited mentorship, and inadequate basic support affect school attendance and confidence.

Programs

Focused support where it matters most.

School Supplies

Exercise books, writing materials, and essential learning items for children who need support staying in school.

Mentorship

Motivational talks and guidance sessions that help children build confidence, discipline, and hope for the future.

Hygiene Support

Sanitary pads and basic hygiene items to reduce barriers that can interrupt school attendance and dignity.

Community Engagement

Partnerships with families, volunteers, sponsors, and local supporters to keep each drive accountable and effective.

Current project

MW Back-to-School & Empowerment Drive

The first drive will equip vulnerable children with school supplies, hygiene support, refreshments, and mentorship. The expected outcomes are improved school attendance, increased confidence, reduced pressure on families, and a positive community impact.
